How It Works

I come to you.
I set it up.
You keep the time.

This isn't a webinar or a PDF. I come to your business, learn how you work, identify exactly where AI can save you the most time, build your workflows, and train you or your staff to use them. In-person, hands-on, and specific to your business — not a generic template.

01

The Audit

We spend one hour walking through your current process — what you do manually, what repeats, what takes the most time. This is where we identify exactly where AI can have the biggest impact for your specific business.

02

The Setup

I build your custom AI workflows using tools like Claude and ChatGPT — automated follow-ups, lead responses, content calendars, proposal drafts, and more. No tech knowledge needed on your end.

03

The Training

I train you and your team in-person until you're confident. You'll walk away knowing exactly how to use your new tools, what they can do, and how to get more out of them over time.
