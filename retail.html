What I Automate For You

Six workflows that keep your
shop top of mind.

🏷️

Product Description Writer

Give AI your product name, category, and a few key details. It writes polished, engaging, SEO-friendly descriptions in seconds — for one item or fifty at a time.

Replaces: an hour of writing for every new arrival

📧

Email Newsletter

Share your current inventory highlights and upcoming promotions. AI drafts a complete, on-brand newsletter — subject line, body copy, and call to action — ready to send in minutes.

Replaces: the email list you have but never use

📅

Monthly Social Calendar

One planning session generates 30 days of Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest content — tied to your products, seasonal moments, and local community — ready to post and schedule.

Replaces: inconsistent posting that loses you followers

⭐

Review Response Drafts

Every Google review — a glowing thank-you or a tough complaint — gets a professional, personalized draft response. You approve with one click. Your reputation stays active.

Replaces: reviews going unanswered for months

🎄

Seasonal Campaign Copy

Mother's Day. Summer sales. Back to School. The holidays. Each occasion gets a complete copy package — email, social posts, and in-store signage text — drafted weeks in advance.

Replaces: last-minute scrambling before every big shopping moment

💬

Customer Inquiry Responses

Common questions about hours, returns, gift wrapping, or availability get instant, accurate draft responses. You review and send. Customer service stays fast even on your busiest days.

Replaces: typing the same answers to the same questions
