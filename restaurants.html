What I Automate For You

Six workflows that keep
guests coming back.

⭐

Review Response Drafts

Every new Google and Yelp review — positive or negative — gets a thoughtful, on-brand draft response. You review and publish with one click. Your online reputation stays active.

Replaces: reviews sitting unanswered for weeks

📅

Monthly Social Calendar

One session with your menu and upcoming events generates 30 days of Instagram and Facebook posts — photos cued, captions written, ready to schedule. A full month in an hour.

Replaces: scrambling for content ideas after a long service

🍽️

Catering Inquiry Response

Every catering or private event inquiry receives an immediate, professional response with your packages, minimums, and availability — within minutes, any time of day.

Replaces: private event leads going cold overnight

Weekly Specials Email

You send a short list of this week's specials. AI drafts a polished email to your guest list — with the right tone, enticing descriptions, and a reservation link — in minutes.

Replaces: the weekly email you keep meaning to send

Google Business Q&A

Customers post questions to your Google Business profile constantly — hours, parking, allergen info. AI drafts responses so nothing goes unanswered and your listing stays fresh.

Replaces: unanswered questions that cost you reservations

Event & Holiday Campaigns

Valentine's Day prix fixe. Mother's Day brunch. New Year's Eve. Each occasion gets a complete campaign — email, social posts, and caption copy — drafted and ready weeks in advance.

Replaces: last-minute scrambling before every holiday
