What I Automate For You

Six automations for the
business you're too busy to run.

📲

Missed Call Text-Back

Every missed call instantly receives a personalized text — your name, your business, your availability — within seconds. Leads stay warm while you stay on the job.

Replaces: losing $45K–$120K/year in missed inquiries

📋

Estimate Builder

Speak your job notes into your phone. AI formats them into a professional, itemized written estimate ready to send — in under two minutes, from the job site.

Replaces: an hour of typing every evening after work

⭐

Review Request Sequence

24 hours after every completed job, a personalized review request goes out automatically. More 5-star reviews. Zero extra effort.

Replaces: the review ask you always forget to make

📅

Appointment Confirmations

Customers receive automatic confirmation and a reminder 24 hours before their appointment — reducing no-shows without you making a single reminder call.

Replaces: calling every customer the day before

Seasonal Campaigns

Spring AC tune-ups. Pre-winter plumbing checks. Holiday landscaping. A custom email or text campaign drafts itself and goes out to your list at exactly the right time.

Replaces: the seasonal outreach that never happens

Photo-to-Scope

Upload a photo of the job site or damage. AI drafts a plain-English scope of work — materials, labor description, timeline — that you can send as-is or adjust.

Replaces: writing scope of work from memory each time
